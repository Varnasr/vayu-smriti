## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- Respectful, constructive communication
- Factual accuracy and intellectual honesty
- Crediting others' work appropriately
- Focusing on the public interest mission
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them contribute
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Prioritizing what is best for affected communities and the archive

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, or ad hominem arguments
- Partisan political advocacy or campaigning
- Deliberate misinformation or misrepresentation of sources
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sexual language or imagery in project spaces
- Other conduct inappropriate for a professional, public-interest project

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Vayu Smriti project spaces, including:

- GitHub repository (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Project communication channels
- Events and meetings affiliated with the project
- Representation of the project in public spaces

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Violations may be reported to: **conduct@vayusmriti.org**

All reports will be reviewed confidentially. Include:
- Your contact information
- Names/usernames of those involved
- Description of the behavior
- Any supporting documentation

### Response

Project maintainers will:

1. Acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
2. Review the report and gather additional context if needed
3. Determine appropriate action
4. Communicate the outcome to relevant parties

### Consequences

Depending on severity, responses may include:

- **Correction:** Private clarification of why behavior was inappropriate
- **Warning:** Formal warning with consequences for continued behavior
- **Temporary ban:** Temporary removal from project spaces
- **Permanent ban:** Permanent removal from project spaces
